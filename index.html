About

In late January of 1989 Jean Carlo was born. It was a hot summer night in Brazil and after a long cesarian Jean was delivered.

A health baby boy who broght with him joy and happiness for his also new born parents.

A happy baby who became a happy child, who became a wonderfull teenager and later a brilliant man.

At the age of fourteen he was the only kid of his class being accepted to entry a prestiged high school programme which was integrated with a eletronic tecnhician course in his county

When he gradueted from school, he started straight away a Job in the biggest open tv channel in Brazil, in the meanwhile he was also starting his eletronic engineering degree.

After a long time in this secure and confortable position, he decided it was time to take another path and against any prediction he took front of his life and start his onw business in a complete diferent field.

Currently, Jean's a son, a brother, a husband, a father, a godfather, a friend, a business men, a cheff. But more important than all, today he is happier than he have never been before.

I am no longer in his everyday projects, but I coundn't be prouder of the man he became, I love you, I miss you, you are my best friend and the person I mostly admire in the role world.
